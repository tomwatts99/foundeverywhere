The problem

Referrals are great. Until they are not enough.

Referrals are the lifeblood of most professional services firms. They come with trust built in and they convert well. But they are unpredictable, they do not scale, and they dry up when you need them most.

The firms growing consistently are the ones that show up when a potential client searches for help. Not just on Google - on ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini too. When someone asks an AI tool to recommend a solicitor, accountant, or consultant in their area, the firms that appear are the ones that have built the right signals. Most have not.

Referral dependency

When referrals slow down there is no independent channel to compensate. Growth becomes unpredictable.

Invisible to active searchers

Clients searching online for professional services find whoever ranks. If that is not you, it is a competitor.

Not appearing in AI recommendations

AI tools are increasingly the first port of call for professional recommendations. Most firms are absent entirely.

The opportunity

High intent. High value. High conversion.

Someone searching for a solicitor, accountant, or consultant is not browsing. They have a problem and they need help. That intent makes professional services one of the highest-converting categories in local search. The firm that appears first - in Google, in Google Maps, in AI-generated answers - captures that enquiry. The ones that do not appear miss it entirely.

What we do

Everything a professional services firm needs to be found

  • 01

    Local SEO

    We optimise your website and local presence so you appear when someone in your area searches for the services you offer. The right keywords, the right structure, and the right technical foundations to capture high-intent local searches.

  • 02

    Google Business Profile

    Your GBP is often the first thing a potential client sees. We optimise every element - categories, services, photos, posts, and attributes - and manage it on an ongoing basis so it stays current and competitive.

  • 03

    AI and LLM visibility

    When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity to recommend a solicitor, accountant, or consultant in their area, we make sure your firm appears. Schema markup, entity signals, and structured content that gives AI systems what they need to cite you confidently.

  • 04

    Review generation and management

    Reviews build trust and drive rankings. We implement a review generation process that reaches clients at the right moment, monitors incoming reviews, and helps you respond effectively to build a strong public reputation.

  • 05

    Expert content

    Content that demonstrates your expertise and experience - not generic articles but pieces that only your firm could write. The kind of content Google rewards and AI systems cite because it provides genuine insight that goes beyond the obvious.

  • 06

    Technical SEO and performance

    A slow or poorly structured website undermines everything else. We audit and fix your technical foundations - PageSpeed, Core Web Vitals, schema, and on-page SEO - so your site supports your visibility rather than holding it back.

Common questions

What professional services firms ask us

We get most of our work through referrals. Do we really need SEO?

Referrals are valuable but they are not predictable or scalable. SEO and AI visibility build a channel that generates enquiries independently of your referral network - so when referrals are quiet, the phone still rings.

How long does it take to see results?

Technical fixes and GBP improvements can show within weeks. Local organic rankings typically improve over two to three months. AI visibility improvements can show within weeks as systems re-crawl and update.

Do you understand the compliance constraints around marketing professional services?

Yes. We understand that solicitors, financial advisers, and regulated professionals have constraints on claims and testimonials. We work within those constraints and flag anything that needs compliance review before it goes live.

Can you help us appear in AI tools like ChatGPT?

Yes. AI visibility is a core part of everything we do. We implement the schema, entity signals, and content structure that give AI systems what they need to recommend your firm when someone asks for professional services in your area.

We already have a website and it ranks reasonably well. Is there still value in working with you?

Almost certainly yes. Most sites that rank reasonably well in Google are still invisible in AI search. Schema is often incomplete, AI visibility has never been tested, and there are usually technical improvements that would push rankings further. Get in touch and we will tell you honestly what the opportunity looks like.

Do you work with regulated professions like solicitors and financial advisers?

Yes. We are familiar with the marketing constraints that apply to regulated professions and we work within them. All content and claims are agreed with you before anything goes live.
