Why we started this

The SEO industry was slow to notice that search had fundamentally changed. We were not.

We started Found Everywhere because we kept seeing the same problem. Businesses investing in websites and SEO, then watching AI tools send their customers to competitors who had never even heard of schema markup. The search landscape changed faster than most agencies noticed.

Most SEO agencies are still optimising for 2019. They chase Google rankings while ignoring the fact that a growing slice of their clients' customers never see a search results page at all. They just ask ChatGPT. We think that is a problem worth solving properly.
