The problem

Marketplace dependency is a slow leak

Most automotive businesses know this feeling. Lead costs creep up year on year. Auto Trader changes its algorithm or its pricing and enquiries dip. The business owns very little of its own demand and has limited ability to change that quickly.

The businesses winning in automotive search right now are not the ones with the biggest marketplace budgets. They are the ones that showed up consistently in local search, built trust through reviews, and made sure Google and AI tools could find them when a buyer was ready.

Rising acquisition costs

Marketplace fees increase every year. Cost per lead climbs. Margins compress.

No ownership of the relationship

The marketplace owns the customer data. You get the enquiry, not the relationship.

Zero control over demand

When the platform changes, your leads change. You have no independent channel to fall back on.

The opportunity

Local search is the channel you own

Most automotive buyers start their search online. Google Maps and local results drive first contact. A buyer searching for a used car dealer in their town will call the business that appears at the top of local results - not necessarily the one with the biggest Ad budget or the most marketplace listings. Local visibility is one of the few scalable channels a dealer owns outright.

What we do

Everything a dealer needs to own local search

  • 01

    Local SEO and rank tracking

    We optimise your site for local search and track exactly where you appear across your town or city - not just a single average position but a geo-grid showing your visibility street by street. You always know where you are winning and where there is ground to gain.

  • 02

    Google Business Profile

    Your GBP is the single most important local ranking asset you have. We optimise every element - categories, services, photos, posts, attributes, and Q and A - and manage it on an ongoing basis so it stays current and competitive.

  • 03

    Citation management

    Consistent business information across hundreds of online directories is a core local ranking signal. We audit your existing citations, fix inconsistencies, and build new listings so search engines find the same accurate information about your business everywhere it appears.

  • 04

    Review generation and management

    Reviews drive both rankings and buyer trust. We implement a review generation process that reaches customers at the right moment, monitors incoming reviews across all major platforms, and displays your best reviews on your website automatically.

  • 05

    Reputation management

    We collect internal feedback before directing customers to leave a public review. This means your best experiences reach Google while giving you the chance to resolve issues directly with the customer before they go public.

  • 06

    AI and LLM visibility

    Automotive buyers are increasingly using AI tools to research and shortlist businesses. We make sure your dealership appears when someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity to recommend a dealer in your area - with the schema, entity signals, and structured data that make that possible.

Common questions

What automotive businesses ask us

Can you work alongside our existing Auto Trader or marketplace activity?

Yes. We are not anti-marketplace - they serve a purpose. We work alongside your existing activity to build the organic and local channel so you are less dependent on any single platform over time.

How long does it take to see results in local search?

GBP improvements can show within weeks. Local organic rankings typically improve over two to three months as citations are built and the site is optimised. Review volume builds steadily from the first month.

Do you work with multi-site dealer groups?

Yes. Multi-site groups get a scoped programme that covers all locations with consistent management and consolidated reporting.

Can you help us get more Google reviews?

Yes. Review generation is included in the monthly management service. We implement a process that reaches customers at the right moment and makes it easy to leave a review.

How do you track local rankings for a dealership?

We use geo-grid rank tracking that shows your visibility across your town or city at a granular level - not just a single average position. You can see exactly where you appear and where competitors are outranking you.

Do AI tools recommend local businesses like car dealers?

Increasingly yes. When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity to recommend a dealer in their area, these systems pull from structured data, reviews, and local signals. We make sure your business sends the right signals to appear in those recommendations.
