FAQItem

What is LLM visibility, exactly?

It's the practice of making sure your brand, product, and expertise are surfaced inside AI answer engines — ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Copilot. The work overlaps with SEO but isn't the same: citations, entity clarity, and factual coverage matter as much as keywords.

Is this in addition to SEO or instead of it?

In addition. Classic search isn't going anywhere — but the new answer engines are taking a growing share of high-intent queries. We treat both as one system.

How long does it take to see results?

Technical fixes show in Google within 2–6 weeks. LLM citations move faster on some indexes and slower on others — usually a 4–12 week horizon for clear lift.
