What the audit covers

  • 01

    Technical health

    Crawlability, indexing, redirects, broken links, duplicate content, and the technical foundations that search engines need to work properly. Most sites have more issues here than they realise.

  • 02

    PageSpeed and Core Web Vitals

    We test your site on both mobile and desktop and score every Core Web Vitals metric. Slow sites rank lower and convert worse. We tell you exactly what is causing the problem.

  • 03

    On-page SEO

    Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, keyword usage, and internal linking. We check every indexable page and flag what needs fixing.

  • 04

    Schema and structured data

    We audit your existing schema implementation and identify every opportunity to add structured data that helps search engines and AI systems understand your content.

  • 05

    Mobile experience

    More than half of all searches happen on mobile. We test your site as a mobile user and flag anything that is hurting your visibility or your conversions.

  • 06

    AI visibility check

    Every audit includes a test of your site across the major AI systems - ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Copilot. You will know exactly where you stand before we start any work.

Common questions

What people ask about the audit

What do I need to provide?

Just your website URL and a brief description of your business and target customers. We handle everything else.

Will the audit tell me what to fix or will you fix it too?

The audit is a standalone service - you get a full report and fix list you can act on yourself or hand to a developer. If you want us to do the fixes, we can quote for that separately or move onto a retainer.

My site was built by an agency. Can you still audit it?

Yes. We audit sites built on any platform - WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, Shopify, custom builds. The platform does not matter.

How is this different from a free SEO tool?

Free tools check a limited set of signals and give you a score. We check 40+ technical factors, test your AI visibility across five platforms, explain what every issue actually means, and tell you what to fix first. The output is actionable, not just informational.

Do you audit ecommerce sites?

Yes. Ecommerce audits cover everything in the standard audit plus product page optimisation, category structure, and schema for products and reviews.

What happens after the audit?

That is entirely up to you. Some clients take the report and act on it themselves. Others ask us to do the fixes. Others move straight onto a retainer. There is no pressure to do anything beyond the audit.
