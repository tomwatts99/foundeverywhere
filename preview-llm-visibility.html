What is included

Everything your site needs to be found by AI

01

Schema markup implementation

We implement comprehensive structured data across your site: Organisation, WebSite, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, and Article schema. This is the primary signal AI systems use to understand what your business does and whether to cite it.

02

llms.txt setup

A plain text file at your domain root that tells AI crawlers exactly what your site is about and which content is most useful to index. Several major AI systems already check for this. We write it, structure it correctly, and keep it updated.

03

Entity and brand signal audit

AI systems think in entities, not just keywords. We audit your brand presence across the web (consistency of your business name, description, and details across every platform) and fix the gaps that make AI systems uncertain about recommending you.

04

AI-ready content structure

We restructure your key pages so they answer questions directly and clearly. Question-led headings, concise answers at the top of sections, FAQ blocks with schema: the format AI systems extract from when building their answers.

05

AI citation monitoring

We test your brand across the major AI systems before and after the work. You will see exactly where you appear, where you do not, and how that changes over time.

Common questions

What people ask about LLM visibility

What is LLM visibility?

LLM visibility means your website appears as a cited source when people use AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Gemini to search for your services. These systems pull from specific sources when building their answers. We implement the signals that make your site one of those sources.

How is this different from normal SEO?

Traditional SEO optimises for Google's ranking algorithm. LLM visibility optimises for how AI systems understand, trust, and cite content. The signals overlap but they are not the same. Schema markup, entity consistency, and question-led content structure matter far more for AI visibility than they do for traditional rankings.

How long does it take to see results?

Schema and llms.txt changes can be picked up by AI crawlers within days. Appearing consistently in AI-generated answers typically takes four to eight weeks as the systems re-index and update their responses.

Can you guarantee we will appear in ChatGPT?

No, and anyone who says they can is not being straight with you. What we can guarantee is that we implement every known signal correctly and test your visibility before and after. The AI systems make their own decisions, but right now most sites are not even trying. We make sure yours is.

Do we need this if we already rank well in Google?

Yes. Google rankings and AI citation are separate. A site can rank first in Google and be completely absent from AI-generated answers. The signals are different enough that one does not guarantee the other.
