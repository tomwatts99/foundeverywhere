Do I need to have done an audit first?

Not necessarily, but it helps. If we have not worked together before we will do a quick review of your site before starting a retainer to make sure we know exactly what we are working with.

What is the minimum commitment?

Three months. SEO and AI visibility work takes time to show results and three months gives us enough time to do meaningful work and show you the impact.

Can I cancel after three months?

Yes. After the initial three months the retainer runs month to month with 30 days notice to cancel.
