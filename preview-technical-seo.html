Technical SEO

The foundations everything else sits on

Most SEO problems are technical problems. Slow pages, crawl errors, broken structure, missing schema. We find them, fix them, and make sure they stay fixed.

Get your free audit ↗ See all services

What we fix

  • 01

    Crawl and indexing

    We make sure search engines can find, crawl, and index your site correctly. Blocked pages, noindex tags, redirect chains, and crawl budget issues — all identified and resolved.

  • 02

    Core Web Vitals

    Google uses Core Web Vitals as a ranking signal. We test your LCP, CLS, and INP scores, identify exactly what is causing them to fail, and fix the underlying issues rather than just treating the symptoms.

  • 03

    Site architecture

    A logical, flat site structure helps search engines understand your content and distribute authority effectively. We review your URL structure, category hierarchy, and navigation and fix what is not working.

  • 04

    Internal linking

    Internal links tell search engines which pages matter. Most sites have orphaned pages, broken links, and missed opportunities to pass authority to their most important content. We fix all of it.

  • 05

    Page speed

    Beyond Core Web Vitals, raw page speed affects both rankings and conversions. We optimise images, eliminate render-blocking resources, implement caching, and strip out anything slowing your site down.

  • 06

    Schema and structured data

    Structured data helps search engines and AI systems understand your content. We implement the right schema types for your business and make sure they validate correctly.

Why it matters

You cannot build on broken foundations

Content, links, and strategy all matter. But none of them work properly if your technical foundations are broken. A slow site loses rankings before the content even gets a chance. A page Google cannot crawl does not exist. Schema errors mean AI systems cannot understand your business. Technical SEO is not the exciting part of the job. It is the part that makes everything else work.

Common questions

What people ask about technical SEO

Do you fix the issues or just report them?

We fix them. Every technical SEO engagement includes hands-on implementation. You get a report of what we found and a fixed site, not a to-do list.

What platforms do you work with?

Any platform — WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, Shopify, Astro, custom builds. If it has a URL we can audit and fix it.

How long does technical SEO take to show results?

Some improvements show within days — particularly crawl and indexing fixes. Core Web Vitals improvements typically show in Google Search Console within two to four weeks. Ranking improvements follow over the subsequent weeks as Google re-crawls and re-evaluates the site.

My site already has an SEO plugin. Do I still need this?

Yes. SEO plugins handle on-page basics like title tags and meta descriptions. They do not fix Core Web Vitals, crawl errors, redirect chains, site architecture problems, or structured data implementation. Those require hands-on technical work.

Will this affect my existing content or design?

No. Technical SEO work happens under the hood. Your content and design stay exactly as they are. We only touch the technical layer.

How do I know if my site has technical SEO problems?

Almost every site does. Common signs include slow PageSpeed scores, pages not appearing in Google, rankings that have dropped without explanation, and Core Web Vitals failures in Search Console. Get in touch for a free audit and we will tell you exactly what your site needs.
